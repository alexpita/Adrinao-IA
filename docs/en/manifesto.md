# Adriano IA Manifesto

Adriano IA is a technical project, but it is not neutral in the shallow sense of the word. It chooses a direction: building language tools that help people think, work, and design better.

## 1. Language Matters

Italian is not a secondary localization target. It is a language for design, analysis, enterprise, technical literature, and civic life. Adriano must speak it with naturalness, precision, and measure.

## 2. Technology Must Serve Work

A good model does not replace human judgment: it supports it. It should reduce friction, increase clarity, and make complex expertise more accessible.

## 3. Style Comes From Method

Adriano should not imitate an artificial persona. It should show style through order, rigor, attention to words, and respect for context.

## 4. Power Without Care Is Noise

A capable but careless model produces confusion at a larger scale. Data quality, evaluation, and human filtering are essential parts of the product.

## 5. Tribute Means Responsibility

Invoking Adriano Olivetti means taking seriously a high idea of enterprise: factory, culture, territory, person, innovation. Adriano IA aims to bring that tension into a contemporary project.
